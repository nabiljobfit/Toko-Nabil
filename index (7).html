About Us

Welcome to Toko Putra Barokah

Toko Putra Barokah is your one-stop shop for quality eyewear, raincoats, watches, and clothing. Established in 2025, we have been serving our customers with premium products at affordable prices.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products that combine style, functionality, and durability. We carefully select each item in our inventory to ensure customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the leading retailer of quality lifestyle products in Indonesia by 2030.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality products at competitive prices
  • Deliver excellent customer service
  • Continuously expand our product range
  • Maintain ethical business practices

Our Journey

2025

Establishment

Toko Putra Barokah didirikan dengan toko kecil di Yogyakarta, khusus menjual eyewear dan jas hujan.

2026

First Expansion

Memperluas lini produk untuk mencakup jam tangan dan membuka outlet kedua di Surakarta.

2027

Online Presence

Meluncurkan website e-commerce dan aplikasi mobile untuk melayani pelanggan secara nasional.

2028

Clothing Line

Memperkenalkan lini pakaian pria dan wanita, berekspansi ke 5 outlet di Jawa Tengah.

2029

National Recognition

Menerima penghargaan "Retailer Terbaik Tahun Ini" dari Asosiasi Retail Indonesia.

2030

Regional Expansion

Membuka outlet internasional pertama di Singapura, menandai masuknya ke pasar Asia Tenggara.
